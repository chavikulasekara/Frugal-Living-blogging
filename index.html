Frugal Living in Urban Settings

Urban Frugal Living is a resource for city dwellers looking to live on a budget. It offers tips on finding affordable housing, maximizing small spaces, and maintaining homes cost-effectively. It also covers urban transportation, grocery shopping, growing food, and budget-friendly meals. The guide also covers reducing utility bills, finding affordable healthcare, and DIY projects.
